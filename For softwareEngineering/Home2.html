CARING FOR THE ELDERLY : An Overview of Aged Support Systems:

United Nations published an article on Ageing in the Twenty-First Century (2012), thus defining an ageing populace as an occurrence in which older people become a proportionally larger aspect of the total population because of decline in fertility and increase in life expectancy. Almost every country in the world is experiencing this situation including Malaysia. This circumstance has caused among others the problem of dependency which affect many sectors including the labour and financial market. “Dependency” in this context means individuals who require someone or something for financial or other supports. In Order to reduce the level of stress for older citizens which require services such as shopping,provision of meal, transportation and health care, we will like to create an information system that can match service providers with seniors who require such service. With the help of the system, these seniors will be able find information and use the services available
